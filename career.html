Principal Investigator-Clinical

Hyderabad
3+ Years

The Principal Investigator (PI) in Clinical Research is responsible for the overall conduct of clinical trials, ensuring the integrity of the study, adherence to regulatory requirements, and the safety and well-being of participants.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and refine clinical study protocols
  • Ensure study design aligns with regulatory guidelines
  • Oversee the ethical conduct of clinical trials
  • Lead multidisciplinary teams in executing clinical trials
  • Manage study budgets and timelines
  • Maintain compliance with Good Clinical Practice (GCP)

Qualifications:

  • MD, DO, PhD, or equivalent in a relevant field
  • Extensive experience in clinical research and trial management
  • In-depth knowledge of GCP and regulatory requirements
  • Strong leadership and team management skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
